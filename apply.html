This is Your Credit Union

For over 85 years, the Library of Congress Federal Credit Union has proudly served America’s library community. We’re here to help you write your financial story. To get started, join the Credit Union that exclusively serves the library community.

Your membership supports the library community

We believe the library community advances America's great story. When you join, we’ll donate $25 to support one of the funds, endowments or programs from the list. Make your choice with the I Want to Join the Credit Union form below.

21st Century Fund: this fund gives the ALA flexibility to respond to unanticipated challenges and act on new opportunities.
ALA General Scholarship Endowment: created to offer ongoing support of all ALA scholarship programs.
Library of Congress National Book Festival: an annual festival that celebrates the joy of reading and the creativity of America’s authors, illustrators, and poets.

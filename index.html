August 1, 2022

Library of Congress Accepting Applications for Of the People: Widening the Path Grant Funding

Learn more

The Library of Congress has opened the second round of grant funding to individuals and organizations focusing on the cultures and traditions of diverse communities across the United States. Up to 10 grant opportunities are available for up to $50,000 each. Applications will be accepted through August 1.
